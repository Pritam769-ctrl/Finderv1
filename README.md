# üì± Finder App - User Manual

**Finder** is your personal, offline digital organizer. It helps you remember where you put things by storing photos, videos, locations, and notes directly on your device.

This guide covers every feature of the app to help you get the most out of it.

---

## üì• How to Install (PWA)
Finder is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. This means you can install it on your phone without using an App Store, and it works 100% offline.

### For iOS (iPhone/iPad)
1. Open the app link in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom.
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add**. The app will appear on your home screen like a native app.

### For Android (Chrome)
1. Open the app link in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three dots** (menu) in the top right corner.
3. Tap **"Install App"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Install**.

> **Note:** Once installed, you can turn off your Wi-Fi/Data, and the app will still work perfectly!

---

## ‚ûï Adding Items

To store something in Finder:
1. Tap the big blue **(+)** button at the bottom right.
2. **Add Media:** Tap the large upload box to take a photo/video or select one from your gallery. You can select multiple files at once.
3. **Name:** Give your item a name (e.g., "Passport").
4. **Location:** Write where it is (e.g., "Top drawer, blue folder").
5. **Category:** Select a tag (e.g., Documents). You can create a new category by tapping **"New"**.
6. Tap **"Save Batch"** to finish.

### üìã Paste from Clipboard
If you just want to save text (like a tracking number or address):
1. Tap the **Clipboard icon** button (next to the plus button).
2. The app will automatically paste the text from your clipboard and save it into the "Texts" category.

## üîç Browsing & Searching

### Home Screen Navigation
* **Search Bar:** Type a name, location, or date to filter items immediately.
* **Categories:**
    * Tap a pill (e.g., "Electronics") to see only those items.
    * **Swipe Feature:** Swipe your finger **Left** or **Right** anywhere on the screen to quickly switch between categories (e.g., slide from "All" to "Favorites").
* **Sorting:** Tap the "Newest/Oldest" button to change the order.
* **View Mode:** Tap the Grid/List icon (top right) to toggle between big thumbnails or a detailed list.

---

## üõ† Managing Items

Tap on any item to view its details.
* **Pin:** Keeps the item at the very top of your list.
* **Favorite:** Adds the item to the "Favorites" category.
* **Share:** Sends the text or image to other apps (WhatsApp, Email, etc.).
* **Copy:** Copies the note/text to your clipboard.
* **Download:** Saves the photo or video back to your phone's gallery.
* **Edit:** Lets you change the photo, name, or location.
* **Delete:** Moves the item to the Recycle Bin.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Settings Menu

Access Settings by tapping the **Gear icon** in the top right corner.

### üé® Appearance
* **Dark Mode:** Toggle this switch to turn the background black (easier on the eyes at night).

### üè∑ Manage Categories
* Tap the **"Manage Categories"** bar to expand it.
* **Rename:** Tap the **Pen icon** to change a category name (e.g., change "Keys" to "Security").
* **Delete:** Tap the **Trash icon** to remove a category.
    * *Safety Note:* Items in a deleted category are **not** lost; they are moved to "Others".

### üíæ Storage
* This bar shows how much space Finder is using on your phone.
* It automatically switches units (KB, MB, GB) based on usage.
* Since Finder uses database storage, you can store as many photos/videos as your phone's memory allows.

### üóë Recycle Bin
* Items deleted from the home screen go here.
* **Restore:** Puts the item back on your home screen.
* **Delete Forever:** Permanently removes the item.
* **Empty Bin:** Deletes everything in the bin at once.
* *Auto-Delete:* Items left in the bin for more than **30 days** are automatically deleted to save space.

### ‚òÅÔ∏è Data Management (Backup & Restore)
**Important:** Because Finder is offline, your data lives in your browser. If you clear your browser's cache "Site Data", you will lose your items. **Use Backup regularly.**

1.  **Backup (Download):** Creates a file (`.json`) containing all your photos, videos, and notes. Save this file to your phone or Google Drive.
2.  **Restore (Merge):** Tap this to select a backup file you previously saved. It will add those items to your current app.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Reset App
* This button wipes **everything**. All photos, notes, and settings will be deleted. Use this only if you want a fresh start.
